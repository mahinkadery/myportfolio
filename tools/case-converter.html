FAQs

Does Sentence Case handle punctuation?

Yes! Our Sentence Case logic looks for periods, exclamation marks, and question marks to determine where a new sentence begins, ensuring proper capitalization after each one.

What happens to numbers and symbols?

Numbers and special characters (like @, #, $) are left unchanged, as they do not have upper or lower case variants. Only alphabetic characters are affected.

Can I convert code variable names?

While this tool is designed for natural text, you can use "lowercase" to normalize variable names. For specific code casing like `camelCase` or `snake_case`, check out our developer-focused tools.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use Title Case for Impact: Studies show that headlines in Title Case draw more attention than those in sentence case.
  • Don't Abuse Uppercase: WRITING IN ALL CAPS IS OFTEN PERCEIVED AS YELLING. Use it sparingly for emphasis, not for entire paragraphs.
