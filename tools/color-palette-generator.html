What Makes a Good Color Palette?

A well-designed color palette typically includes:

  • Primary Colors: Main colors that define the brand or design.
  • Secondary Colors: Supporting colors that complement the primary palette.
  • Accent Colors: Colors used for emphasis and calls-to-action.
  • Neutral Colors: Grays, blacks, and whites for text and backgrounds.
  • Varied Tones: Different shades and tints to provide depth and flexibility.
