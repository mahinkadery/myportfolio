PDF to DOCX FAQs

Is this PDF to DOCX converter free to use?

Yes, our PDF to DOCX converter is completely free to use. There are no signups required, no hidden fees, and no account creation needed. Simply upload your PDF and convert it to Word format.

Will the converted DOCX keep the same layout?

Our converter makes every effort to preserve the original formatting, layout, and structure of your PDF document. However, complex layouts, custom fonts, or images may require manual adjustments in the Word document after conversion.

Is it safe to upload sensitive documents?

Yes. Files are processed securely through our automation workflow and are not stored permanently on our servers. Your documents are deleted shortly after conversion is complete, ensuring your privacy and data security.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required. This converter works entirely in your web browser. All you need is a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge, and an internet connection.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes, the converter is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. You can upload PDFs and download converted DOCX files directly from your smartphone or tablet browser.

What is the maximum file size I can convert?

The maximum file size for conversion is 10 MB. This limit ensures fast processing times and reliable conversions. If your PDF is larger than 10 MB, consider splitting it into smaller files or compressing it before conversion.
