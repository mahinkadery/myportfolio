Game Strategy

While the game is based on random chance when playing against a truly random opponent, understanding probabilities can help:

  • Equal Probability: Each choice has an equal chance of winning
  • No Optimal Strategy: Against random play, all choices are equal
  • Pattern Recognition: Against humans, look for patterns (though the computer is random)
